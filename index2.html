The Problem

In India, more than 18,000 villages and 235 Mn people live without electricity.
The abundance of it is under-appreciated in the urban areas whereas the rural ones have to work way too hard for it.
The reliability of the other renewable sources is low due to climatic, geographic, monetary, amongst many other constraints.

We generate clean energy without harnessing solar, wind, hydro, tidal or biomass!

The Idea

We have devised a disruptive system for generating clean electricity which is more reliable & affordable than other renewable sources.

PRINCIPLE

The machine is being designed and simulated to harness the principle of gravitational force acting downwards along with buoyant force acting upwards to make a sustainable system which is reliable, easy to install & maintain.

FUNCTIONALITY

The gravitational force drives down the pre-decided load of mass and this action exerts an equivalent torque on the attached shaft which is efficiently utilized into driving electric generators.

OUTPUT

The output of this machine is clean, and cheap electricity with more than 70 % capacity factor.

Gravity

Power of falling !!

During Han Dynasty in China, trip hammers powered by the gravity-driven water wheel were used to pound and hull grain, break ore and in the early papermaking. Since the 19th-century gravity is being used to generate electricity as on hydropower plants.

Inspired by making our system 100% eco-friendly, we are harnessing energy from the work done by nature: Gravity.

Buoyancy

Floating bodies, Eureka !!

Buoyancy as discovered by Archimedes in 265 BCE, is the uplift force exerts when an object of given mass is immersed in the fluid.

A practical application can be seen in modern-day submarines.

Applying the similar concept of buoyancy we ideated our concept to be complete with it.
